  • Stop Loss: Spend >$50 + 0 conversions in 5 days → auto-disable + email alert

Priority order (highest impact first):

  1. Audience
  2. Offer / Message angle
  3. Creative (video vs. image vs. carousel)

A/B Testing Framework

Test ONE element at a time.

Priority order (highest impact first):

  1. Audience
  2. Offer / Message angle
  3. Creative (video vs. image vs. carousel)
  4. Hook (first 3s / first line)
  5. CTA
  6. Headline
  7. Colors / Visual palette

Min $50 spend + 5 conversions per variant · Run 7 days minimum

Monday Review Checklist (Every Monday 9am)

  • Review total spend for the week vs. plan (±10% acceptable)
  • CPL by campaign and adset — compare against benchmark
  • Lead volume by day — identify peak and low days
  • Frequency by adset — alerts if >3.5
  • CTR by ad — pause any <0.8% with enough data
  • Adsets in "Learning" — do not touch until out of learning phase
  • Top 3 ads of the week — document why they worked
  • Bottom 3 ads — pause or test new variant
  • Review Google negative keywords (new search terms)
  • Lead quality score (sales team closing rate follow-up)
  • Adjust budget based on performance (scale winners, cut losers)
  • Plan 2–3 new creatives for the following week
